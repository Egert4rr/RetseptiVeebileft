Lasagne

  • Ground meat - 1.5 kg
  • Lasagne sheets - 20 pc
  • Tomato sauce - 1.5 kg
  • Grated cheese - 500 g

Preperation

  1. Cook the ground meat in a pan
  2. Add the tomato sauce to the cooked ground meat, mix and cook
  3. Prepare and oven pan with cooking paper
  4. Add a layer of 5 lasagne sheets and cover it with the sauce, repeat this until you are out of sauce or sheets
  5. Put the oven to cook from both sides and on 180 degrees
  6. Put your lasagne into the oven for 30 min
